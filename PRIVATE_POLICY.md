# Privacy Policy - EchoTrace

**Effective Date:** February 2, 2026

## Data Collection
EchoTrace collects only web history data: domain names and time 
spent on each site. No personal information, content, or metadata.

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device. 
No data is transmitted to servers or third parties.

## User Control
You can view, export, or delete all data anytime via the dashboard.

## Permissions
- tabs: Extract domain from active tab
- storage: Save tracking data locally
- idle: Detect AFK periods
- alarms: Periodic background updates
- history: Reserved for future enhancement (not active in v1.0)
